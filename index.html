Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FortuneShade. Still have questions? Contact our support team.

Is FortuneShade suitable for complete beginners?

Absolutely! FortuneShade is designed to support traders at all experience levels. Our comprehensive curriculum starts with the fundamentals and gradually progresses to advanced concepts. Complete beginners receive extra support through our new trader onboarding program and dedicated beginner-friendly community channels.

How much capital do I need to start trading?

We recommend starting with at least $5,000 for futures trading and $2,000 for forex trading to properly implement our risk management principles. However, new members can begin with our paper trading simulator until they're confident in their abilities and have sufficient capital. We emphasize capital preservation and sustainable growth rather than risky strategies.

What markets do you focus on?

FortuneShade specializes in futures trading (including ES, NQ, CL, GC), major forex pairs, and select commodities. Our methodology is market-agnostic and can be applied across different assets, but we focus on liquid markets with ample trading opportunities. The principles you'll learn can be applied to other markets as well.

What times are the live trading sessions?

We host live trading sessions during the most active market hours: 8:30 AM - 11:30 AM ET (New York Open) and 2:30 AM - 5:30 AM ET (London Open). All sessions are recorded and available for replay in our members' area, so you can learn and benefit regardless of your time zone. Additionally, we provide daily market analysis and trade setup videos for Asian sessions.

How long does it typically take to become profitable?

While individual results vary based on commitment, learning pace, and psychological factors, most dedicated members start seeing consistent results within 3-6 months of following our program diligently. Trading is a skill that requires practice and discipline. We focus on long-term development rather than promoting unrealistic "get-rich-quick" expectations. Our most successful members commit to daily learning and deliberate practice.

Can I cancel my membership if it's not right for me?

Yes, you can cancel your membership at any time. Monthly and quarterly plans automatically renew until canceled. To cancel, simply log into your account dashboard and click the "Cancel Membership" button, or contact our support team. We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new members if you feel our program isn't the right fit for you.
